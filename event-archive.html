Past Limited Events (No Longer Active)

These events have ended. Items exclusive to them are no longer obtainable unless the event returns (some may come back annually). Use this page to identify vintage collectibles and to know which rewards you may have missed.

⚠️ Permanently Retired Items

These items were exclusive to certain events and have been marked “Vintage” by the developers. They have never returned and likely never will. Owning them is a mark of veteran status.

  • Alpha Test Participation Trophy (pre‑launch event)
  • Founder’s Outfit (early supporter pack, not from seasonal event)
  • “First Snow” Wreath (Winter Lights 2023, different from later version)

🏷️ Identifying Archived Items

  • In your inventory, hover over an item. If it says “Event Item” and the event name is grayed out, it is from a past event.
  • Items from ended events cannot be traded, sold, or deleted (they remain in storage).
  • Some archived items have a “Vintage” tag in their description.

If you missed an event, keep an eye on “Flashback” weekends – developers occasionally bring back one old item for a limited time.
