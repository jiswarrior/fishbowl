Level Up Faster with Focus

Focus is your most powerful tool for rapid skill progression. By keeping your Focus bar high and using the right foods, you can double or even triple your XP gains. This guide shows you how to maintain an optimal Focus loop, which activities yield the most XP per Focus point, and how to combine buffs for record‑breaking leveling speeds.

🧮 The Focus Multiplier

Total XP = Base XP × (1 + Current Focus %)

Where Focus % = (Current Focus / Max Focus) × 100

Example 1: Base XP = 100, Max Focus = 150, Current Focus = 120 → 80% bonus → 180 XP.
Example 2: Same base, Focus = 0 → 0% bonus → 100 XP.

The higher your Focus percentage, the more XP per action. Thus, never let Focus drop to zero if you want to level quickly.

📈 Increasing Max Focus Capacity

Your starting Focus cap is 100. Every time you increase it, the same amount of Focus points gives you a higher percentage bonus. Spend Renown at the Phoenix Shrine (Kilima Valley) to raise your cap.

🚀 Fast Leveling Routes per Skill

🌱 Gardening

Plant the highest XP crops: tomatoes (repeat harvest) and cotton. Water twice daily. Use speed‑grow fertilizer. Mass‑produce seeds at the seed maker for bonus XP.

🎣 Fishing

Use glow worms in Bahari Bay at night to catch rare fish (Midnight Carp, Storm Eel). Each rare catch gives massive XP. Fish in groups – more fish spawn.

⛏️ Mining

Focus on iron nodes in Bahari Bay. Smelt ore into bars for additional XP. Use a pickaxe with “Rare Find” enchant to spawn extra nodes.

🏹 Hunting

Hunt Sernuk in the Kilima outskirts. Aim for headshots to kill faster. Use slowdown arrows to prevent escaping. Group hunting increases spawn rate.

🐞 Bug Catching

Catch fireflies and glow bugs at night. Use smoke candles to avoid scaring them. The Bahari beach at dusk has rare bugs.

🪑 Furniture Making

Craft the highest tier furniture you can (iron/palium). Each piece gives huge base XP. Build multiple workbenches to craft in parallel.

✨ Stackable XP Buffs

  • Focus Bonus % upgrade – At the Dragon Shrine, you can increase the maximum XP multiplier from 100% to 200% (costs Renown). This is the single best long‑term investment.
  • Skill Boosts from NPCs – Talking to a guild NPC daily sometimes gives a 30‑minute 20% XP boost for that skill.
  • Furniture set bonuses – The “Cozy Cottage” set increases Focus restoration by 5% when placed in your home.
  • Event buffs – Limited‑time events (like Maji Market) often have food that gives +50% XP for an hour.
💥 Max XP scenario: 200% Focus bonus + 20% NPC boost + 50% event food = 270% extra XP. That’s 3.7x normal XP!

📅 Weekly Strategy for Fast Leveling

  • Complete all weekly challenges – They reward Skill Coins that can be exchanged for XP‑boosting items (e.g., Fertilizer, Smoke Candles, Arrows).
  • Use Skill Coins for recipes – Unlock the Seed Maker, Preserves Jar, and Sawmill early to process materials for bonus XP.
  • Group up – Joining a community (guild) gives access to shared resource nodes and group XP bonuses.
  • Focus on one skill per session – Don’t spread Focus across many activities; depleting Focus on low‑yield actions wastes potential.
