Help Us Build the Ultimate Resource

Palia Guide Hub thrives on community knowledge. If you’ve discovered a great strategy, created a beautiful home layout, or have deep expertise in a skill, we want your contribution. This page explains how to submit your work for inclusion – you’ll get full credit and a permanent spot in the guide.

📝 What You Can Submit

  • Written guides – Step‑by‑step tutorials for skills, quests, money making, romance, etc.
  • Farming & home layouts – Detailed descriptions with images or diagrams.
  • Data tables / spreadsheets – Comprehensive lists (e.g., full fish spawn times, gift preferences).
  • Video guides (embedded) – YouTube or Twitch links with written summary.
  • Interactive tools / calculators – If you can provide JavaScript code snippets.
  • Translations – Help us support more languages.

📋 Submission Requirements

  • Original content – Do not copy from other websites or claim others’ work as your own.
  • Clear and accurate – Information should be up‑to‑date (based on current Palia patch).
  • Proper formatting – Use headings, bullet points, and images if possible. Plain text is also accepted; we will format it.
  • Images / screenshots – Preferably high‑resolution, hosted on Imgur or similar. Include captions.
  • Contact info – Provide your in‑game name or Discord tag so we can credit you.

📬 How to Submit

Option 1: Email
Send your submission to contributions@paliaguidehub.com (placeholder; replace with actual address). Attach images or link to documents.

Option 2: Discord
Join our community Discord (invite link on main page) and post in #submissions channel. Ping an editor (@EditorTeam).

Option 3: Google Form
Use our submission form (link below). Allows file uploads up to 100MB.

Option 4: GitHub (for developers)
If you’re comfortable with Markdown/HTML, you can fork our repository and create a pull request. See the GitHub repo for details.

⏳ Review Process & Timeline

  1. Initial review by editor (1–3 business days).
  2. If accepted, you’ll receive a confirmation and possible request for minor edits.
  3. Guide is formatted and published on the appropriate sub‑page.
  4. You’ll be notified when it’s live and given a direct link.
  5. Major updates to your guide can be submitted as revisions.

Guides that are incomplete or off‑topic may be declined. You are welcome to resubmit after improvements.

🏆 Contributor Rewards

  • Credit line – Your name (and social link if desired) will appear at the top of the guide.
  • Contributor badge – On your profile within our Discord community.
  • Featured placement – Exceptional guides may be highlighted on the homepage.
  • Monetary reward – For very large or complex submissions (e.g., complete interactive calculator), we offer a small honorarium (discussed case by case).

✅ Do's and Don'ts of Submissions

DO:
  • Write in clear, simple English (translations welcome).
  • Include screenshots for visual guides.
  • Test your instructions in the current game version.
  • Cite sources if you use community data.
  • Be respectful and constructive.
DON'T:
  • Plagiarize from official wiki or other sites.
  • Submit low‑effort or one‑sentence tips.
  • Include exploits or cheats.
  • Post affiliate links or ads.
  • Submit the same guide to multiple channels.

📢 Questions? Talk to an Editor

If you’re unsure whether your idea fits, feel free to reach out before writing a full guide.

  • Discord: @EditorTeam (in #submissions channel)
  • Email: editors@paliaguidehub.com
  • Reddit: DM u/PaliaGuideHub
