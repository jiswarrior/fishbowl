📌 About the Calculators

  • Fish Timer: Averages from player‑reported data. Multiply by 1.5 if unlucky.
  • Crop Growth: Times are real minutes; use the datetime picker to see harvest countdown (based on your local time).
  • Friendship Meter: Assumes one gift per day. Does not include weekly want bonuses or quests.
  • All calculators are client‑side – your data never leaves your device.
