Plan Your Day in Palia

Many fish, bugs, and resources only appear at specific in‑game times or seasons. This guide provides real‑world time conversions, optimal hunting windows, and seasonal spawn tables so you never miss rare catches.

💡 Tip: Open the in‑game clock (top‑left of screen) to see the current time. Set a timer on your phone for 24 minutes to track full cycles.

🌸 Seasonal & Weather-Based Spawns

Palia has four seasons (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter) that rotate every real‑world week. Each season brings unique fish, bugs, and foragables.

🌼 Spring

  • Rainbow Butterfly (daytime)
  • Morel Mushrooms
  • Spring Carp (fish)

☀️ Summer

  • Firefly (night)
  • Watermelon (crop)
  • Sunfish (morning)

🍂 Fall

  • Moonstruck Moth
  • Pumpkin (crop)
  • Salmon migration

❄️ Winter

  • Frost Beetle
  • Ice Fishing: Frostfang
  • Holly Berries

Weather also matters: rain increases spawn rates of rare fish (Storm Eel) and some bugs (Rainbow Butterfly). Check the in‑game weather icon.

⏰ Reset Schedules – Don't Miss Out

  • Daily Reset: 4:00 AM UTC – respawns all resources, gifts, and shop items.
  • Weekly Reset: Monday 4:00 AM UTC – new weekly challenges and Maji Market if active.
  • Season Change: Every Monday (same as weekly reset) – new season begins.
  • Maji Market (event): Runs for two weeks, then 2‑week break. Check official news.
