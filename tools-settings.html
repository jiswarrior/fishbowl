⚙️ Graphics & Performance Tuning

🖥️ Low‑End PCs (Integrated graphics, 4‑8GB RAM)

  • Resolution: 720p or 900p
  • Windowed mode (Fullscreen may cause lag)
  • Graphics Quality: Low
  • Shadows: Off
  • Anti‑aliasing: Off
  • View Distance: Medium (or Low)
  • V‑Sync: Off
  • FPS Cap: 30 or 60 (test which is stable)
  • Close background apps (browsers, Discord).

💻 Mid‑Range PCs (GTX 1060 / RX 580, 16GB RAM)

  • Resolution: 1080p
  • Graphics Quality: Medium
  • Shadows: Low
  • Anti‑aliasing: FXAA or Low
  • View Distance: High
  • V‑Sync: On (to prevent screen tearing)
  • FPS Cap: 60 or Unlimited

🚀 High‑End PCs (RTX 2060+, 32GB RAM)

  • Resolution: 1440p or 4K
  • Graphics Quality: High / Epic
  • Shadows: High
  • Anti‑aliasing: TAA or High
  • View Distance: Maximum
  • FPS Cap: 144 (if monitor supports)

🎮 Nintendo Switch (Docked / Handheld)

  • Game automatically adjusts; no manual graphics settings.
  • For better performance: Close other software, play in Airplane Mode (offline), lower TV output to 720p if docked, and restart the console before playing.
  • Reduce in‑game camera sensitivity (Settings → Controls → Camera Sensitivity = 0.4–0.6).

🌐 Network Optimization & Ping Reduction

  • Wired connection: Use Ethernet instead of Wi‑Fi for more stable ping.
  • Close bandwidth‑heavy apps: Streaming video, large downloads, or torrents.
  • Select the correct region in the launcher (NA, EU, Asia).
  • DNS settings: Use Cloudflare (1.1.1.1) or Google (8.8.8.8) for faster resolution.
  • Port forwarding: Not typically required; but if you experience constant disconnects, forward UDP ports 27000–27030 to your PC.
  • Switch users: Place console closer to router; restart network settings via “Initialize Console” (does not delete save data).

🎛️ Recommended Game Settings (Quality of Life)

📷 Camera & Controls

  • Field of View (FOV): 90–100 (wider view, less motion sickness)
  • Mouse Sensitivity: 0.7 (default) – adjust to your comfort
  • Invert Y‑Axis: Personal preference
  • Controller Vibration: Off (improves battery life on Switch and reduces distraction)

🔊 Audio

  • Master Volume: 70% (avoid missing quest dialogue)
  • Music Volume: 40% (ambient, not overwhelming)
  • SFX Volume: 80% (important for fishing bite sounds)

🖥️ Display

  • Fullscreen Mode: Fullscreen (better performance than Windowed)
  • Resolution Scale: 100% (reducing causes blur)
  • Brightness: 1.2 (if game appears dark)

🎮 Gameplay

  • Auto‑Sprint: On (toggle sprint with Shift)
  • Toggle Tool Wheel: On (press Q once instead of holding)
  • Show Player Names: Always (helpful for grouping)
  • Quest Tracker: Keep at least 3 quests pinned

🔧 Advanced Tweaks (Config Files – PC Only)

For experienced users, you can manually edit the GameUserSettings.ini file located in %LOCALAPPDATA%\Palia\Saved\Config\WindowsClient\.

  • Disable motion blur: bMotionBlur=False (under [ScalabilityGroups])
  • Reduce particle effects: sg.EffectsQuality=1 (instead of 3)
  • Increase texture streaming pool: r.Streaming.PoolSize=2000 (higher for VRAM >6GB)

⚠️ Editing config files may cause crashes if misconfigured. Back up the original file first.

✅ Quick Setup Checklist

  • Set graphics preset according to your hardware (Low/Medium/High)
  • Enable Auto‑Sprint and Toggle Tool Wheel for comfort
  • Adjust FOV to 95 for better peripheral vision
  • Connect via Ethernet if possible, close background apps
  • Remap hotkeys that feel awkward – especially tool wheel if you use mouse side buttons
  • For Switch, lower camera sensitivity to 0.5 and play in Docked mode for stability
