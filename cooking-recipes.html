💰 Most Profitable Dishes for Selling

  1. Celebration Cake (★) – 900 gold, high ingredient cost but best profit per dish.
  2. Blueberry Pie (★) – 450 gold, simpler ingredients.
  3. Mushroom Risotto (★) – 300 gold, easy to mass-produce if you grow rice.
  4. Hearty Vegetable Soup (★) – 270 gold, great for early game.
  5. Apple Pie (★) – 375 gold, requires apple trees (take time to grow).

For pure gold per hour, focus on Celebration Cake if you have blueberries and wheat. Otherwise, Hearty Vegetable Soup is the best balance of ingredients and profit.
