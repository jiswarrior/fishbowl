⭐ Star Quality Crops

  • Star quality (★) crops sell for 50% more than normal.
  • Using Quality Fertilizer increases star chance to ~40% base.
  • Higher Gardening skill (level 10+) gives passive +10% star chance.
  • Star crops also produce star seeds in Seed Maker, which grow into star crops more often.
  • Star crops are required for certain cooking recipes to yield star dishes.
💡 Pro tip: Save star crops for cooking (they boost dish quality) and sell regular crops directly.

🌱 Seed Maker Efficiency

Unlocked at Gardening Level 8. Input one crop to receive multiple seeds (2-5 depending on crop).

  • Best crops to turn into seeds: Carrot (yields ~3 seeds, each costs 5g → profit), Tomato (yields 1-3 seeds, valued 10g each).
  • Star crops yield more seeds – Star tomato gives 2-4 seeds instead of 1-3.
  • Processing time: 10-25 minutes per batch. Multiple Seed Makers speed up production.
