Special Seasonal Celebrations

Throughout the year, Palia hosts limited‑time events with unique activities, exclusive rewards, and festive decorations. This guide covers the three major recurring events – Maji Market (summer), Winter Lights (winter holidays), and Halloween. Mark your calendar so you don’t miss them!

🎡 Maji Market (Summer Carnival)

When: Typically runs for 2 weeks in June/July. Exact dates announced on official Discord.

Where: Special night‑time map accessed via a portal near Kilima Town Hall.

How to join: No level requirement – available to all players.

Activities:

  • Fortune Telling (earn Maji Coins)
  • Chappa Chase (catch glowing chapaas)
  • Fireworks Show (daily at 21:00 in-game time)
  • Merchant Stalls (buy exclusive furniture, outfits)

🎁 Notable Rewards:

  • Maji Firefly Lantern (housing decoration)
  • Carnival Outfit Set (top, pants, hat)
  • Fortune Pig Plushie
  • Maji Market Music Box (plays event theme)

❄️ Winter Lights (Holiday Event)

When: December 15 – January 5 (real‑world dates).

Where: Snowy variant of Kilima Valley (temporary reskin).

How to join: Automatically active; talk to “Holiday Herald” near the inn.

Activities:

  • Gift Exchange Board (give gifts to NPCs for Winter Tokens)
  • Ice Fishing (catch Frost Eel, Glacial Salmon)
  • Snowball Fight (emote game with other players)
  • Decorate the Town Tree (collective community event)

🎁 Notable Rewards:

  • Winter Lights Wreath (door decoration)
  • Frost Deer Mount (statue for home)
  • Cozy Winter Sweater (cosmetic)
  • Holiday Stockings (wall hanging, 4 variants)
  • Frost Eel (trophy fish for aquarium)

🎃 Halloween (Spooky Festival)

When: October 20 – November 3 (real‑world dates).

Where: Special “Haunted Bahari” zone – accessed via a portal in Bahari Bay.

How to join: Speak to “Spooky Zeki” near the Bahari entrance.

Activities:

  • Candy Corn Hunt (collect from special pumpkins)
  • Ghost Bug Catching (catch translucent insects)
  • Haunted Maze (navigate a spooky labyrinth)
  • Costume Contest (NPC judges best outfits)

🎁 Notable Rewards:

  • Spooky Lantern (glowing ghost decoration)
  • Witch’s Hat (cosmetic)
  • Pumpkin Patch (crop plot reskin)
  • Ghostly Plushie (companion decoration)
  • Candy Corn Soup recipe (cooking)

💡 Event Participation Tips

  • Log in daily – Most events have daily bonuses (extra tokens, special quests).
  • Focus on one event at a time – If multiple events overlap (rare), prioritize the one ending sooner.
  • Save your tokens – Don’t buy cheap items early; save for the high‑value limited items (outfits, epic furniture).
  • Group up – Some activities (like Haunted Maze) are easier with friends.
  • Check official announcements – Event dates sometimes shift. Join the Palia Discord for reminders.

🔮 Future Events (Speculated)

  • Spring Blossom Festival (April) – rumored flower planting event with new gardening rewards.
  • Anniversary Celebration (August) – special login rewards and returning old event items.
  • Maji Market may return twice a year (summer and winter variant).

Follow official news to stay informed. Events are free for all players.
