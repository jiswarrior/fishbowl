📅 Weekly Challenges – Gold & Renown Rewards

⚡ Efficient Weekly Challenge Route

To maximize gold per time, prioritize challenges that overlap with your daily routine.

  • Monday reset: Check all guild challenges first. Focus on the ones that reward extra Renown or Skill Coins.
  • Combine activities: Forage while mining, fish while waiting for crops, hunt during night cycles.
  • Use processing stations: Smelt bars while you fish – they complete passively.
  • Don’t force rare challenges: If a challenge requires an epic fish, skip it unless you already have it. The time spent is not worth the gold.
  • Complete at least 3 challenges per skill – the bonus chest is the biggest reward.

🎣 Semi‑AFK Fishing (Glow Worm Farming)

While not fully AFK, fishing with glow worms can be done while watching a movie. Set up at a high‑yield spot (e.g., Bahari Lake at night).

  • Investment: 100 glow worms (crafted from glow bugs and plant matter).
  • Return: Average 5000–8000 gold per 100 casts (including rare fish).
  • Time: ~30 minutes active, but you can click while doing other things.
  • Best spot: Bahari Lake, 0:00–3:00, use glow worms for Midnight Carp (500g each).

🎁 Daily Login Bonus & Streak

Logging in every day gives increasing rewards:

  • Day 1: 100g
  • Day 2: 150g
  • Day 3: 200g
  • Day 4: 300g
  • Day 5: 400g
  • Day 6: 500g
  • Day 7+: 500g + 10 Renown

That’s up to 3500g per week just for logging in.
