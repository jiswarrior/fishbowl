Your First Steps in Palia

When you first arrive in Palia, you'll start from a cozy camp. This guide walks you through customizing your character, unlocking essential tools, claiming your home plot, and mastering the fast travel network — so you can explore Kilima Valley and Bahari Bay with ease.

🎨 Character Creation

  • Appearance Customization: Choose hairstyle, hair color, skin tone, eye shape, facial details. You can change some aspects later in-game (may cost Renown).
  • Starter Outfits: Several basic color sets are available; more outfits can be obtained from events, the store, or Renown rewards.
  • Character Name: Unique and permanent (choose wisely). It appears in friend lists and the community.
  • Tutorial: After creation, a short story quest teaches basic controls (movement, interaction, tool wheel).
💡 Tip: There are no races or classes — you can learn every skill freely, so just pick the look you like.

🏡 Home Plot Unlock & Expansion

  • ✔ First Home Plot: Complete the early main quest "Your Little Plot of Land" – talk to the town hall NPC to get your first housing foundation for free.
  • ✔ Layout & Building: Place your house, workshops, animal barns, crop fields, and decorations. Initial build limits increase as you gain Renown.
  • ✔ Buying Additional Plots: Purchase extra plots (e.g., second farm area, orchard) from the town hall with gold. Prices range from 2,000 to 10,000 gold.
  • ✔ Decoration & Moving: Press 'H' to enter build mode and freely move structures. Most furniture can be crafted at workbenches or bought from stores.
🏠 Pro tip: Your home plot has a fast travel point (Home Shrine) that you can use to return at any time.

⛩️ Fast Travel Network

Major areas in Palia have Fast Travel Boards (standing wooden signposts). Once activated, you can instantly teleport between them for a small gold fee (or sometimes free).

  • Kilima Valley: Town Hall Square, Fisherman's Dock, Central Orchard, Mountain Lookout.
  • Bahari Bay: Beach Entrance, Mining Camp, Ruins, Lighthouse.
  • Your Home Plot: Always available at your housing entrance.
  • Fast Travel Costs: 10-50 gold for same-map travel; 50-100 gold for cross-map (Valley ↔ Bahari). Completing certain NPC quests can unlock discounts.
First return home each day is free
In a party, you can use your leader's travel boards
