7‑Day Roadmap

From complete beginner to skilled crafter & entrepreneur

Back to Main Hub

Your Guided Path to Mastery

This roadmap breaks your first week into daily goals. Follow it step by step, and you’ll build a solid foundation in skills, gold, relationships, and home expansion. Adjust the pace to your playtime – each day represents roughly 2–4 hours of gameplay.

Day 1 – Settling In

  • Create character & complete tutorial – Learn movement, tool wheel, and basic interactions.
  • Unlock fast travel – Activate the board at Kilima Village square.
  • Gather your first resources – Pick up 50 wood, 30 stone, and 20 fiber.
  • Craft a workbench – Place it on your home plot.
  • Meet the key NPCs – Talk to Hodari (mining), Ashura (fishing), and Badruu (gardening).
  • Goal: Reach level 2 in Mining and Foraging. Earn ~500 gold.
  • End of day: Unlock your first backpack upgrade (500 gold → 25 slots).

Day 2 – First Tools & Gardening

  • Upgrade pickaxe & axe to copper – Mine copper ore and smelt bars at the furnace.
  • Unlock gardening – Complete Badruu’s quest to receive your first soil plot.
  • Plant tomatoes and carrots – Water them daily; tomatoes give best early profit.
  • Start cooking – Build a campfire and cook grilled meat for Focus restoration.
  • Increase Focus capacity – Spend your first Renown at the Phoenix Shrine (focus +10).
  • Goal: Reach level 3 Gardening, level 3 Mining. Save 2000 gold.

Day 3 – Fishing & Bug Catching

  • Get the fishing rod – Quest from fisherman Einar. Fish at Kilima River for common fish.
  • Catch 10 different bugs – Use the free bug net. Nighttime bugs sell well.
  • Complete first weekly challenges – Check the “Accomplishments” tab.
  • Expand your garden – Buy second soil plot from Badruu (1000 gold).
  • Goal: Reach level 3 Fishing, level 3 Bug Catching. Earn 3000 gold.
  • Tip: Sell fish to Einar for slightly better prices than Zeki.

Day 4 – Home Expansion & Hunting

  • Build a sawmill – Process wood into planks for better furniture.
  • Buy a new room – Add a hallway or medium room to your house (5000 gold).
  • Start hunting – Get the bow from Hassian. Hunt Sernuk near the outskirts.
  • Cook Hearty Vegetable Soup – Best early Focus food (requires carrot, onion, mushroom).
  • Goal: Reach level 4 Hunting, level 4 Gardening. Save 5000 gold.
  • Social tip: Give simple gifts to one NPC (e.g., Hodari likes stone).

Day 5 – Iron Tools & Bahari Bay

  • Unlock Bahari Bay – Follow the main quest to the new zone.
  • Mine iron ore – Found in Bahari cliffs. Upgrade pickaxe to iron (level 5 mining).
  • Catch rare fish – Use glow worms (crafted from compost) for Midnight Carp.
  • Start preserving – Build a preserves jar and turn tomatoes into pickled tomatoes (higher profit).
  • Goal: Reach level 5 Mining, level 5 Gardening, level 5 Fishing.
  • Renown upgrade: Spend another 20 Renown on Focus capacity (now 120 max).

Day 6 – Relationships & Passive Income

  • Reach friendship level 2 with two NPCs – Give loved gifts (check wiki for favorites).
  • Set up seed maker – Turn star‑quality crops into seeds (sell extra seeds).
  • Buy a second backpack upgrade – 1000 gold for 30 total slots.
  • Complete the “Temple of the Waves” puzzle – Rewards rare furniture and Renown.
  • Goal: Passive income of 2000 gold/day from crops and preserves.
  • Optional: Start romancing one NPC (requires level 3 friendship + gift).

Day 7 – Mastery & Endgame Preparation

  • Reach level 10 in Gardening or Mining – Unlock the skill’s special reward (e.g., Greenhouse recipe).
  • Craft your first piece of epic furniture – Use iron and palium (if found).
  • Join a community (guild) – Group up for flow tree farming and rare hunts.
  • Save 10,000 gold – For the next backpack upgrade or a new house room.
  • Explore the Maji Market (if active) – Earn event tokens for exclusive items.
  • Reflect on progress: You now have 7+ skills at level 5+, a growing home, and steady gold income. Ready for any content Palia offers!

🚀 What’s Next After Day 7?

  • Focus on one skill to level 25 – Unlock silver achievements and exclusive titles.
  • Complete all temple puzzles – There are three main temples with unique rewards.
  • Decorate your home to 3000+ decoration points – Unlock special furniture sets.
  • Become a millionaire – High-end furniture crafting and jam production.
  • Help new players – Share resources and build the community.
