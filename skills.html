7 Core Skills in Palia

Mastering these skills is essential for crafting, earning gold, and progressing through the game. Each skill has its own experience bar, tools upgrades, and unique rewards. Below you'll find everything you need to know about Gardening, Fishing, Mining, Hunting, Foraging, Bug Catching, and Furniture Making.

Gardening

  • Unlock: First farming quest (early)
  • Tools: Watering Can (upgrades at lv5, 10, 15)
  • Main activities: Plant seeds, water, harvest crops
  • Pro tip: Use fertilizer for star-quality crops; focus on high-profit crops like tomatoes or cotton.

Fishing

  • Unlock: Fisherman NPC quest
  • Tools: Fishing Rod (upgrades at lv5, 10, 15)
  • Main activities: Catch fish from rivers, ponds, and ocean
  • Pro tip: Use different baits (worm, glow worm) for rare fish; fish during specific times.

Mining

  • Unlock: After tutorial, get Pickaxe
  • Tools: Pickaxe (upgrades at lv5, 10, 15)
  • Main activities: Mine stone, clay, copper, iron, palium
  • Pro tip: Higher tier pickaxe lets you mine rare nodes faster; focus on Bahari Bay for iron/palium.

Hunting

  • Unlock: Join Hunting Guild (early access)
  • Tools: Bow (upgrades at lv5, 10, 15)
  • Main activities: Hunt deer, chapaa, sernuk, and magical creatures
  • Pro tip: Aim for headshots; use smoke bombs to scare prey towards you.

Foraging

  • Unlock: Automatically available
  • Tools: None (just pick up)
  • Main activities: Gather mushrooms, berries, sweet leaf, wild herbs
  • Pro tip: Foraging nodes respawn daily; focus on rare mushrooms (morel, truffle) for high gold.

Bug Catching

  • Unlock: Quest "Introduction to Entomology"
  • Tools: Bug Net (upgrades at lv5, 10, 15)
  • Main activities: Catch butterflies, beetles, glow bugs
  • Pro tip: Use smoke candles to avoid scaring bugs; rare bugs appear at night or during rain.

Furniture Making

  • Unlock: Build Workbench at home
  • Tools: Workbench, Sawmill, etc.
  • Main activities: Craft furniture from wood, stone, fiber, and rare drops
  • Pro tip: High-level furniture can be sold for good gold or gifted to NPCs for friendship points.

Skill Coins Exchange

Each skill level grants Skill Coins (1 per level after level 5). Exchange them at the corresponding guild store for:

  • Rare recipes / blueprints
  • Exclusive furniture sets
  • Special bait / fertilizer
  • Outfit pieces

Achievements & Hidden Rewards

  • Reaching level 25 in any skill → Silver achievement + title
  • Reaching level 50 → Gold achievement + exclusive emote
  • Hidden rewards: Complete all weekly skill challenges for bonus coins

Fast Leveling Tips

• Keep your Focus bar full (eat cooked food) to gain bonus XP.
• Focus on bulk crafting / farming for XP farming.
• Use experience boosters from the guild store or events.
• Team up for hunting/fishing – shared loot still grants XP.
