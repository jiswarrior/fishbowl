Major Content Updates

Palia evolves with regular patches. This page summarises the most significant updates – from new zones and skills to seasonal creatures and furniture collections. Check back each patch cycle for the latest additions.

🌊 Patch 0.180 – Bahari Bay Expansion

October 2024
  • New map area: Southern Bahari Coast – beachfront with new fishing spots and ruins.
  • New skill: Scavenging (collect hidden treasures, unlock special emotes).
  • New animals: Coastal Crabs (can be caught with bug net) and Sea Turtles (interactive NPC).
  • New furniture set: “Mariner’s Rest” – 12 pieces (ship wheel, anchor, nautical rug).
  • System change: Increased home decor limit from 3000 to 4000.

🌳 Patch 0.175 – Temple of Roots & Flow-Infusion

August 2024
  • New dungeon: Temple of Roots (third major temple) – underground labyrinth puzzle.
  • New mechanic: Flow-Infusion – rare trees/ores spawn at night; drop flow materials for epic crafting.
  • New animal: Flow‑infused Chapaa (magical variant, drops enchanted fur).
  • New furniture: “Rootweaver” set – organic, glowing mushroom furniture (8 pieces).
  • Tool upgrade: Palium Pickaxe now has “Flow Finder” enchant (shows nearby flow nodes on compass).

🎡 Patch 0.170 – Maji Market (Seasonal Event)

June 2024
  • New temporary map: Maji Market – a nighttime carnival zone with games and stalls.
  • New skill activity: “Fortune Telling” – temporary skill (earn Maji Coins).
  • New animals: Maji Fireflies and Fortune Pigs (catchable only during event).
  • New furniture: “Carnival Celebration” set (balloons, booths, rides as decorations).
  • System: Daily event quests resetting at 4:00 UTC.

❄️ Patch 0.165 – Winter Lights (Holiday Event)

December 2024
  • New zone variation: Snowy Kilima Valley (temporary winter reskin).
  • New animal: Frost Deer (huntable, drops winter pelts).
  • New furniture set: “Frostwood Lodge” – 15 pieces (snowy pine, ice chandelier, holiday wreath).
  • New skill bonus: Winter Fishing – increased chance for rare fish (like Frost Eel).
  • System: Gift exchange board – earn Winter Tokens by sending gifts to NPCs.

🌾 Patch 0.160 – Farming Overhaul

May 2024
  • New map: Farming Guild HQ (south of Kilima) – new NPCs and quests.
  • New skill progression: Added Fertilizer Mastery (unlocks at Gardening Lv 20).
  • New animal: Farmable Alpaca (produces luxury wool).
  • New furniture: “Country Harvest” set – wooden barrels, farm carts, scarecrow.
  • System: Seed Maker now accepts all crops; preserve jar production time reduced.
