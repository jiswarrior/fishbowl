🗺️ Best Fishing Spots by Zone

Kilima Valley

  • Fisherman's Pier – Kilima Perch, Bluegill, Dawn Ray.
  • Waterfall Pool – Dawn Ray, Golden Salmon, Sturgeon.
  • Mine Entrance Pond – Sturgeon, Dragon Fish (rare).

Bahari Bay

  • Beach Shore – Bahari Bass, Storm Eel (rain).
  • Central Lake – Midnight Carp, Void Ray.
  • Ancient Ruins Pool – Spectral Fish (pre-dawn).

🎣 Fish Required for Bundles

Several fish are needed to complete the Temple of Waves bundles. Make sure to catch and save these:

  • Dawn Ray – Kilima River, 5:00–8:00, Worm.
  • Midnight Carp – Bahari Lake, 0:00–3:00, Glow Worm.
  • Storm Eel – Bahari Coast, rain only, Worm.
  • Spectral Fish – Ancient Ruins, 3:00–6:00, Glow Worm.

🏆 Pro Tips for Rare Fish

  • Use glow worms for rare/epic fish – They attract higher-tier fish but also increase bite time.
  • Fish in groups – More players fishing the same spot increases spawn rate of rare fish.
  • Check the in‑game clock – Many rare fish appear only during specific 3‑hour windows.
  • Rainy weather is your friend – Storm Eel and some other rare fish only appear during rain.
  • Upgrade your rod – Iron/Palium rods reduce fish struggle time, making it easier to catch legendaries.
