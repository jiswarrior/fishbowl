Guild Representatives & Progression

Each skill is represented by an NPC who offers weekly challenges, sells exclusive items for Skill Coins, and provides valuable tips. Complete their tasks to earn Renown, Gold, and Skill Coins – the currency for rare recipes and cosmetics.

📋 Weekly Challenges (Skill‑Specific)

Each week, every guild offers 3–5 challenges. Completing them awards Renown, Gold, and Skill Coins. Challenges reset every Monday at 4:00 UTC.

💡 Strategy Tips

  • Prioritize weekly challenges – They are the most reliable source of Skill Coins and Renown.
  • Save coins for exclusive items – Don’t waste coins on consumables that can be crafted or bought with gold.
  • Check NPC schedules – Some NPCs sleep at night; you cannot turn in challenges or buy items when they are unavailable.
  • Complete all challenges for a skill – The bonus chest often contains rare items not sold elsewhere.
