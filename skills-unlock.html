🌿 Foraging

  • Lv 2: Better vision for forageables
  • Lv 4: Rare mushrooms appear on map
  • Lv 6: Sweet leaf respawns faster
  • Lv 8: Find truffles (high value)
  • Lv 10: Double gather chance

🐞 Bug Catching

  • Lv 2: Smoke Candle recipe
  • Lv 4: Copper Net (wider capture)
  • Lv 6: Belt Accessory (extra bug slot)
  • Lv 8: Iron Net (longer reach)
  • Lv 10: Palium Net (best capture rate)

🪑 Furniture Making

  • Lv 2: Sawmill (planks)
  • Lv 3: Workbench upgrade
  • Lv 5: Iron Furniture (recipes)
  • Lv 8: Palium Furniture (rare)
  • Lv 10: Guild Store exclusive sets
