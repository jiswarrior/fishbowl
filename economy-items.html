🏪 Selling Tips – Maximize Gold

  • Zeki’s General Store: Buys almost everything at 100% base price. Convenient for bulk selling.
  • Guild Stores: Some items sell for 10% more when sold to the relevant guild NPC (e.g., fish to Einar, crops to Badruu, furniture to Tish).
  • Black Market (Zeki, after hours): Buys rare items (gems, epic fish) for 20% more, but only available at night (20:00–6:00).
  • Don’t sell raw ores – Smelt them into bars first. Bars sell for much more (e.g., 5 copper ore = 50g, 1 copper bar = 25g – actually worse? Wait: 5 ore = 50g, smelt into 1 bar = 25g, so selling ore is better unless you need bars for crafting. So only sell bars if you have excess after crafting.)
💡 Correction: For copper/iron, selling raw ore is more profitable than bars (5 ore = 50g vs bar = 25g). But for palium, bar = 250g vs ore = 50g each (5 ore = 250g, same). So no loss. Always check values.

⭐ Star Quality = +50% Value

Always aim to sell star‑quality items. They are worth 1.5x the base price. Use Quality Fertilizer on high‑value crops (blueberries, tomatoes), and cook with high Focus to increase star chance for dishes.
