Where to Invest Your Resources

Gold and Skill Coins are finite early on. This guide ranks every major purchase by priority – from backpack upgrades and tool upgrades to guild store items – so you never waste currency on things you don’t need. Follow these tiers to accelerate your progression.

🪙 Skill Coin Spending Priority (Per Guild)

Skill Coins are earned from leveling (1 coin per level after Lv 5) and weekly challenges. Spend them on permanent upgrades, not consumables.

Gardening

  • 1️⃣ Seed Maker (30 coins) – essential for crop loops.
  • 2️⃣ Quality Fertilizer recipe (15 coins)
  • 3️⃣ Greenhouse blueprint (50 coins) – endgame.

Fishing

  • 1️⃣ Glow Worm recipe (10 coins)
  • 2️⃣ Fisherman’s Brew (25 coins)
  • 3️⃣ Palium Rod upgrade (60 coins)

Mining

  • 1️⃣ Ore Compressor (20 coins) – compresses ores for higher value.
  • 2️⃣ Palium Detection (40 coins)
  • 3️⃣ “Prospector’s Helmet” cosmetic (75 coins) – optional.

Hunting

  • 1️⃣ Slowdown Arrow recipe (15 coins)
  • 2️⃣ Iron Bow upgrade (35 coins)
  • 3️⃣ “Stalker’s Cloak” (80 coins) – cosmetic.
💡 Never spend Skill Coins on items that can be bought with gold – gold is easier to farm.

🏆 Renown Spending Priority (Shrine Upgrades)

Renown is separate from gold and skill coins. Prioritize these upgrades:

  1. Focus capacity (Phoenix Shrine) – until you have at least 200 max Focus.
  2. Friendship Bonus (Dragon Shrine) – up to +30%.
  3. Focus Bonus % (Dragon Shrine) – increases max XP multiplier from 100% to 200%.
  4. Decor capacity – only if you run out of space.

🚫 Avoid These Gold Wastes

  • Buying seeds from Badruu – instead, use Seed Maker to produce your own from harvested crops.
  • Buying basic resources (wood, stone, fiber) – they are free to gather.
  • Paying for fast travel regularly – walk or use the daily free home return.
  • Cosmetic outfits before essential upgrades – they give no gameplay benefit.
  • Repairing low-tier tools – upgrade instead; repairs are expensive.

📅 Phase‑by‑Phase Summary

  • Early (0–10 hours): Backpack, copper tools, second soil plot, chicken coop.
  • Mid (10–40 hours): Iron tools, barn + cows, seed maker, preserves jar.
  • Late (40+ hours): Palium tools, greenhouse, ore compressor, all skill coin rare blueprints.

Following this priority order, you’ll never feel gold-starved and will progress smoothly.
