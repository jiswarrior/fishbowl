Farm Tokens, Claim Rewards

Each limited event uses its own currency. This guide covers how to earn event tokens efficiently – through daily quests, repeatable activities, and bonus streaks – and what you can exchange them for before the event ends.

🎡 Maji Market – Maji Coins

💰 How to Earn:

  • Daily Fortune Telling (50 coins)
  • Chappa Chase (catch 3 chapaas → 20 coins, repeatable up to 5x/day)
  • Fireworks Show attendance (10 coins daily)
  • Weekly Maji Market challenges (200 coins/week)
  • Rare: Find golden tickets in hidden chests (100 coins each, limited)

🎁 Exchange List (Vendor near Ferris Wheel):

  • Maji Firefly Lantern – 150 coins
  • Carnival Outfit Set – 300 coins
  • Fortune Pig Plushie – 100 coins
  • Maji Market Music Box – 250 coins
  • Firework Launcher (decor) – 80 coins
  • Candy Apple (consumable) – 10 coins

❄️ Winter Lights – Winter Tokens

💰 How to Earn:

  • Gift Exchange Board (give 1 loved gift to NPC → 30 tokens, up to 5 per day)
  • Ice Fishing (each rare fish gives 15 tokens, unlimited)
  • Snowball Fight emote game (play 3 rounds → 25 tokens daily)
  • Decorate the Town Tree (donate 5 wood → 20 tokens, daily)
  • Weekly Winter challenges (250 tokens/week)

🎁 Exchange List (Vendor at Town Square):

  • Winter Lights Wreath – 80 tokens
  • Frost Deer Mount – 250 tokens
  • Cozy Winter Sweater – 120 tokens
  • Holiday Stockings (set of 4) – 200 tokens
  • Frost Eel Trophy – 150 tokens
  • Winter Lantern – 50 tokens

🎃 Halloween – Candy Corn

💰 How to Earn:

  • Candy Corn Hunt (find pumpkins around Kilima – 10 per pumpkin, 5 respawn daily)
  • Ghost Bug Catching (each ghost bug gives 5 Candy Corn, unlimited)
  • Haunted Maze completion (100 Candy Corn first time, 20 repeatable daily)
  • Costume Contest (NPC judges – 50 Candy Corn daily)
  • Weekly Spooky challenges (300 Candy Corn/week)

🎁 Exchange List (Vendor near Haunted Maze entrance):

  • Spooky Lantern – 100 Candy Corn
  • Witch’s Hat – 200 Candy Corn
  • Pumpkin Patch (crop reskin) – 150 Candy Corn
  • Ghostly Plushie – 80 Candy Corn
  • Candy Corn Soup recipe – 50 Candy Corn
  • Halloween Banner – 30 Candy Corn

⚡ Token Farming Efficiency

  • Prioritize daily activities first – Daily quests give the most tokens per minute (e.g., Fortune Telling, Gift Exchange). Do them every day.
  • Repeatable activities after dailies – If you have extra time, focus on the repeatable with best token/time ratio. Example: Ice Fishing (Winter) or Candy Corn Hunt (Halloween).
  • Group up for bonus tokens – In Haunted Maze or Chappa Chase, playing with friends sometimes gives extra tokens.
  • Don’t hoard tokens until last day – Exchange early for high‑priority items in case you run out of time.
  • Check for hidden token caches – Some events have hidden collectibles (golden tickets, secret pumpkins) that give large token sums.

🔄 What Happens to Leftover Tokens?

  • At the end of the event, unused tokens are automatically converted to gold at a rate of 1 token = 2 gold (example: 100 leftover tokens → 200 gold).
  • Some events allow you to exchange tokens for a limited time after the event ends (usually 1 week grace period). Check the event UI.
  • Tokens do NOT carry over to the next event. So spend them before the deadline!

📅 Typical Event Schedule (Real‑World)

  • Maji Market: Late June – Early July (2 weeks)
  • Halloween: October 20 – November 3 (2 weeks)
  • Winter Lights: December 15 – January 5 (3 weeks)

Dates may shift each year. Always check official announcements for exact start/end times.
