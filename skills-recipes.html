Complete Crafting Reference

From planting your first carrot to forging Palium bars and crafting epic furniture, this page lists every known recipe in Palia. Use the tables to quickly find what materials you need and which crafting station to use.

🌱 Seed Recipes (Seed Maker)

All seeds are produced at the Seed Maker (Gardening level 8). Input one crop to get one or more seeds back.

Crop InputSeeds ProducedProcessing TimeNotes
Carrot2–4 Carrot Seeds15 minStar quality gives more seeds
Tomato1–3 Tomato Seeds20 minBest for seed profit
Wheat3–6 Wheat Seeds10 minUsed for flour
Cotton2–4 Cotton Seeds15 minHigh demand for fabric
Rice2–5 Rice Seeds12 minUsed in many dishes
Blueberry1–2 Blueberry Seeds25 minValuable fruit

You can also buy basic seeds from Badruu (Gardening NPC) if you don't have a Seed Maker yet.

⛏️ Ore Smelting & Metal Bars

All smelting is done at the Furnace (Mining level 2). Higher tier bars require better fuels and more time.

Output BarInput OresFuelSmelt TimeSkill Level
Copper Bar5 Copper Ore2 Wood30 secMining Lv 2
Iron Bar5 Iron Ore4 Wood1 minMining Lv 5
Palium Bar5 Palium Ore8 Wood or 1 Coal2 minMining Lv 10
Silver Bar5 Silver Ore6 Wood1.5 minMining Lv 8 (rare find)
Gold Bar5 Gold Ore10 Wood or 2 Coal3 minMining Lv 15
💡 Smelting bars is also a great source of Mining XP – always smelt your ores before selling.
