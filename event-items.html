⚠️ Expiry & Prioritization

  • Check the event end date – Events typically last 2 weeks. The exact date is shown in the event UI. Items disappear from vendors when the event ends.
  • Limited outfits are highest priority – Furniture may return, but costumes rarely do. Get the Witch’s Hat, Carnival Outfit, and Winter Sweater first.
  • Recipe items – If you learn a recipe, you keep it forever. So even if you don’t craft it during the event, buy the recipe before it expires.
  • Event currency farming – Log in daily for bonus tokens. Focus on the most expensive item you want first, then work down.
  • After event ends – Some items may appear in Zeki’s Black Market at a much higher price (e.g., 10,000g for a limited hat). Better to earn during event.

⏱️ How to See Time Left

In‑game, open the Event Panel (calendar icon on the top‑right) during an active event. It shows:

  • Days and hours remaining
  • Current token balance
  • Direct links to event vendors

You can also check official announcements on Discord or the login screen.

📦 What Happens to Expired Items?

Items you already earned stay in your inventory forever – you can still place furniture or wear outfits. However, after the event ends, you cannot buy them again unless the event returns (some do annually). A few items have been marked “Vintage” and never returned.

💡 Pro tip: If you’re unsure, buy at least one copy of every low‑cost item (e.g., plushie, recipe) before the event ends, even if you don’t plan to use it immediately.
