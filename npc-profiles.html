📍 NPC Locations by Zone

Kilima Valley

  • Town Hall / Inn area: Ashura, Reth, Kenyatta, Zeki
  • Farm (southwest): Badruu, Delaila
  • Pier (east): Einar
  • Grove (northwest): Hassian
  • Mines (north): Hodari
  • Forge (central): Sifuu
  • Furniture Shop: Tish

Bahari Bay

  • Alchemist's Hut (south coast): Tamala
  • Fisherman's Dock (east beach): Einar (sometimes)
  • No other NPCs permanently reside in Bahari, but some visit during quests.

🎁 Gift Efficiency Tips

  • One gift per NPC per day – Resets at 4:00 UTC (daily reset).
  • Star‑quality items give +25% extra friendship points. Always give the best version you have.
  • Weekly Wants: Each NPC has 3 random “weekly wanted” items (check their dialogue). Giving a wanted item gives double friendship.
  • Birthday gifts: If you give a gift on an NPC's birthday (check calendar), you get triple friendship.
  • Don’t give hated items – They reduce friendship (-5 points).
