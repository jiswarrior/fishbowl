Building Your Kitchen

To cook advanced dishes, you need proper stations. The Basic Campfire is free, but real cooking starts with the Stove and Oven. This guide covers how to unlock each station, what materials they require, and how upgrading them improves your cooking success rate.

🔥 Basic Campfire

Unlock: Automatic (after tutorial)
Materials: 5 Wood, 5 Stone
Recipes: Grilled Fish, Roasted Mushroom, Campfire Veggies
Limit: No Focus bonus, basic quality only

🍳 Stove – Unlock & Crafting

The Stove is your primary station for soups, stews, stir-fries, and boiling. It requires a workbench and mid-game materials.

RequirementDetails
Skill LevelCooking Level 3
Crafting StationWorkbench
Materials20 Stone, 10 Copper Bar, 5 Iron Bar, 10 Clay
PlacementHome plot (indoors or outdoors)
Recipes UnlockedHearty Vegetable Soup, Sernuk Stew, Mushroom Risotto, Fish Stew, etc.
💡 Tip: Place the Stove near a storage chest for easy access to ingredients.

🥧 Oven – Unlock & Crafting

The Oven is required for baking breads, cakes, pies, and pastries. It becomes available after reaching Cooking Level 5.

RequirementDetails
Skill LevelCooking Level 5
Crafting StationWorkbench
Materials30 Stone, 15 Iron Bar, 10 Copper Bar, 5 Glass, 5 Palium Bar
PlacementHome plot (indoors recommended)
Recipes UnlockedCelebration Cake, Mushroom Quiche, Bread Loaf, Apple Pie, etc.

🥄 Mixer & Prep Station

Some advanced recipes require a Mixer or Prep Station as an auxiliary station. They are not mandatory for basic cooking but enable certain dishes.

Mixer

  • Unlock: Cooking Level 7
  • Materials: 10 Iron Bar, 5 Copper Bar, 5 Glass
  • Used for: Cake batters, dough, sauces

Prep Station

  • Unlock: Cooking Level 4
  • Materials: 10 Wood, 5 Copper Bar, 1 Knife (crafted)
  • Used for: Chopping vegetables, preparing fish fillets

🏠 Placement Tips & Multiplayer

  • Indoor vs Outdoor: Stoves and ovens work anywhere, but placing them indoors (kitchen room) gives a +5% Focus bonus to dishes cooked inside.
  • Group Cooking: If multiple players have upgraded stations, the group can use the highest-level station available. Good coordination reduces material waste.
  • Storage Proximity: Place a storage chest or lockbox next to your cooking area to quickly restock ingredients without running across your plot.
  • Aesthetic Bonus: Some decor items (e.g., hanging herbs, spice rack) provide a hidden +2% to cooking success rate when placed within 5 tiles of a stove or oven.
