Secrets Hidden Across Palia

Palia’s world is filled with hidden collectibles that reward exploration. This guide lists the locations of Silverwing Eggs, treasure chests, lost poems, and other secrets – along with the rewards they unlock (Renown, titles, cosmetics).

🥚 Silverwing Eggs

Silverwing Eggs are rare collectibles hidden in high or hard-to-reach places. There are 15 eggs in total. Finding all of them completes the “Egg Hunter” achievement and unlocks the Silverwing Glider skin.

Reward for all 15: Silverwing Glider skin + “Egg Hunter” title + 200 Renown.

🎁 Treasure Chests (100+ chests)

Scattered across both zones are wooden and iron treasure chests. Each gives gold (50–500g), Renown (10–50), and sometimes furniture blueprints.

  • Kilima Valley hot spots: Behind houses in the village, inside the mine (3 chests), around the central pond (2 chests), on top of the lighthouse.
  • Bahari Bay hot spots: Inside caves (8 chests), on cliff ledges (5 chests), underwater in the bay (3 chests), hidden in ruins (4 chests).

Using an online interactive map is recommended to find all 100+ chests. Reward for opening 50 chests: “Treasure Hunter” title + 100 Renown. For 100 chests: “Master Treasure Hunter” title + Golden Key (cosmetic).

📜 Lost Poems (Scribbled Notes)

There are 12 lost poem fragments hidden around the map. They appear as “Scribbled Note” pickups. Collecting all unlocks a decorative bookshelf for your home.

  • Poem 1: On a bench in Kilima Village square.
  • Poem 2: Inside the library (shelf on the right).
  • Poem 3: On the beach near the pier, under a rock.
  • Poem 4: In the Bahari mines, on a dead‑end ledge.
  • Poems 5–12: Hidden in tree hollows, behind waterfalls, and inside temple puzzle rooms.

Reward: “Poet’s Bookshelf” (furniture) + “Story Seeker” title.

✨ Secrets (Hidden Interactions)

  • Secret Altar: In the Kilima forest, there’s a hidden altar (behind a large tree). Offer 5 sweet leaves to receive a “Forest Blessing” buff (+10% foraging XP for 1 hour).
  • Shrine of the Stars: At Bahari’s highest peak, stand still under the night sky for 10 seconds → “Stargazer” emote.
  • Ancient Music Box: In the Temple of Waves, interact with the broken music box after completing the temple → plays a melody and rewards “Ancient Melody” decoration.
  • Frozen Flower: In the Bahari ice cave (only accessible in winter), pick the rare “Frostbloom” → gives 100 Renown and a temporary cold resistance buff.

🌟 Flow-Infused Trees & Ore

Flow-Infused trees and ore nodes are rare spawns (appear after midnight in Bahari Bay). They drop Flow-Infused Wood and Flow-Infused Ore, which are required for high‑end crafting (Palium tools, epic furniture). Not technically “collectibles” but are hidden in a sense – follow purple glow at night.

🏆 Master Collector Rewards

  • All Silverwing Eggs: Silverwing Glider skin + “Egg Hunter” title
  • All Treasure Chests: “Master Treasure Hunter” title + Golden Key (cosmetic)
  • All Poems: “Poet’s Bookshelf” + “Story Seeker” title
  • All Secrets & Interactions: No single achievement, but unlocks hidden emotes and furniture.

Use online community maps to track your progress – some collectibles are only visible during specific seasons or times of day.
