Master the Rarest Treasures

Legendary fish, epic bugs, palium ore, and flow‑infused trees don’t appear randomly – they follow specific spawn rules. This guide reveals exact time windows, known hot spots, and advanced tricks to make rare farming efficient.

⛏️ Palium Ore

Spawn window: Any time, but higher chance at night (20:00–6:00 in‑game). Also spawns more frequently after daily reset (4:00 UTC).

Known hot spots (Bahari Bay):

  • “Palium Cave” – directly north of the lighthouse, inside the small cave (2–4 nodes).
  • “Waterfall Grotto” – behind the large waterfall in central Bahari (1–2 nodes).
  • “Ancient Ruins Stairs” – at the base of the main ruins staircase (1 node).
  • “Mineshaft 3” – in the deepest part of the Bahari mines (1–2 nodes).

Pro tips: Use a Palium Pickaxe (or at least Iron) to mine faster. If you see a node, mark it on the map and check nearby caves – palium often spawns in clusters within 100 meters.

🐟 Legendary & Epic Fish

🐉 Dragon Fish (Kilima Mines pond)

Time: Any (extremely rare). Bait: Glow Worm. Respawn: Daily reset. Only appears in the deep pond inside the Kilima Mines (east side). Use a Palium Rod and max Focus for best chance.

👻 Spectral Fish (Ancient Ruins pool)

Time: 3:00 – 6:00 (pre‑dawn). Bait: Glow Worm. Respawn: Every in‑game night (if caught, respawns next night). Exact spot: Small pool inside the Temple of Waves outer courtyard.

🌙 Midnight Carp (Bahari Central Lake)

Time: 0:00 – 3:00. Bait: Glow Worm. Best spot: The western edge of the lake, near the small island. Use the “Fisherman’s Brew” buff to increase bite rate.

🐞 Rare Bugs

🌈 Rainbow Butterfly

Time: 10:00 – 14:00. Season: Spring/Summer. Location: Bahari Highlands (eastern cliffs). Trick: Place a smoke candle near flower patches – the butterfly will land and stay longer.

✨ Golden Scarab

Time: 12:00 – 15:00. Season: Summer. Location: Bahari Desert Ruins (southwest). Very rare. Approach very slowly (crouch). If it runs, use a slowdown arrow? No, arrows don’t work on bugs. Use a smoke candle and the master net.

🦇 Vampire Moth

Time: 0:00 – 5:00. Season: Fall. Location: Bahari Caves. Attracted to glow worms placed as bait on the cave floor. Wait 30 seconds after placing bait.

✨ Flow‑Infused Trees & Ore

Spawn: Only at night (20:00 – 6:00 in‑game). Appear as purple‑glowing trees or rocks. They can spawn anywhere in Bahari Bay (rarely in Kilima).

Known clusters: Near the lighthouse (3 trees), behind the waterfall grotto (2 trees), in the northern cliffs (1–2 ore nodes).

Pro tip: Flow‑infused resources require group effort – trees need 2+ players to chop, ore nodes can be mined solo but break faster with group.

⏰ Timing & Weather Overlay

  • In‑game clock: Open the menu to see exact time. Rare spawns are tied to this clock, not real time.
  • Weather effects: Rain increases spawn chance for Storm Eel, Garden Snail, and some rare flowers. Check the weather icon on the map.
  • Daily reset (4:00 UTC): All rare nodes respawn. If you plan a farming run, log in just after reset.
  • Server hopping: If you clear a rare node, logging out and back in might put you in a different server where nodes are still fresh. This is the most efficient way to farm palium and flow trees.

📢 Community Tools for Rare Spawns

  • Palia Discord servers often have dedicated channels like #palium-spawns or #flow-tree-locations where players share coordinates in real time.
  • In‑game chat: Type “/loc” to share your current coordinates. Ask “Any palium/flow up?” in Bahari Bay region chat.
  • Third‑party map apps (e.g., PaliaTracker) allow players to mark rare spawns with timestamps.

Using community callouts can cut your farming time by 80%.
