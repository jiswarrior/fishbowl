🗺️ Best Farming Routes

Kilima Valley (beginners)

  • Copper ore: around cliffs and mines
  • Clay: along riverbanks from Fisherman’s Pier to central pond
  • Sweet leaf & Common Mushroom: fields near the farm
  • Daisies: open grasslands

Bahari Bay (advanced)

  • Iron ore: northern cliffs and caves
  • Palium ore: deep caves (rare), often near waterfalls
  • Spice Sprouts & Cinnamon Bark: highlands and ancient trees
  • Truffles: cave floors (rare spawn)

🔨 Processing Resources

  • Furnace: Copper Ore → Copper Bar (5 ore + 2 wood). Iron Ore → Iron Bar (5 ore + 4 wood). Palium Ore → Palium Bar (5 ore + 8 wood).
  • Glass: 2 Sand + 1 fuel in Furnace (Mining Lv 3).
  • Fabric: 5 Cotton + 1 water in Loom (Gardening Lv 4).
  • Dyes: Crush specific flowers at the Workbench for colored dyes (used in furniture).

🎁 NPCs Who Love Resources

  • Hodari (Mining): Copper Ore, Iron Ore, Palium Ore
  • Badruu (Gardening): Truffle, Morel, Wild Garlic
  • Tamala: Dawn Petal, Glowing Mushroom, Spice Berry
  • Reth: Sweet Leaf, Spice Sprouts, Wild Garlic
  • Tish: Daisy, Bluebell, any flower
  • Ashura: Cinnamon Bark, Wild Berry
