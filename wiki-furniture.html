🔍 Blueprint Acquisition Methods

🏪 In-Game Stores

  • Tish’s Furniture Shop – Sells basic and decorative blueprints (gold).
  • Guild Store (Furniture Making) – Sells advanced blueprints for Furniture Coins.
  • Zeki’s Black Market – Rare, rotating blueprints (high gold cost).

🎁 Quests & Rewards

  • NPC Friendship / Romance levels – Many NPCs give unique furniture as rewards.
  • Temple completions – Each temple rewards a rare decorative item.
  • Treasure chests – Random furniture blueprints can be found in hidden chests.
  • Weekly challenge bonuses – Completing all challenges sometimes yields exclusive blueprints.

🏡 Decor Score & Passive Bonuses

Every furniture piece contributes Decor Points. The total Decor Score of your home plot unlocks bonuses and achievements:

  • 500 points: “Cozy Home” title + 50 Renown
  • 1000 points: “Decor Enthusiast” title + ability to place +10% more items
  • 2000 points: “Master Decorator” title + special flower wallpaper
  • 5000 points: “Architect” title + exclusive gazebo blueprint
💡 Special furniture (like Cozy Fireplace or Homestead Bookshelf) provides active gameplay buffs, not just points. Prioritize crafting these.

🔨 Common Crafting Materials

Wood & Planks: Basic, from trees via Sawmill.
Nails: 1 Iron Bar → 10 Nails (Workbench).
Glass: 2 Sand + 1 fuel (Furnace).
Fabric: 5 Cotton + 1 water (Loom).
Dye: Crush flowers (e.g., Bluebell, Daisy) at Workbench.
Wax: From beekeeping (Beehive, Gardening Lv 6).
Crystal: Rare mining drop (Bahari caves).
Ink: 1 Charcoal + 1 Water (Workbench).

✨ Furniture Set Bonuses

Placing multiple pieces from the same set in one room unlocks additional passive bonuses.

  • Cozy Cottage (3+ pieces): +5% Focus restoration from any food eaten in that room.
  • Ironwood Kitchen (4 pieces): Cooking success rate +10%.
  • Palium Aristocrat (5 pieces): +15% gold from selling crafted items.
  • Gardener’s Corner (3 pieces): Crops in nearby soil plots grow 5% faster.

To see set bonuses, open the room UI (press H) and hover over the “Set Bonus” icon.

✅ Priority Furniture for New Players

  1. Workbench – Required for all crafting.
  2. Sawmill – Turns wood into planks (needed for higher-tier furniture).
  3. Copper Lamp – Easy to craft, gives +5% Focus restoration buff.
  4. Homestead Bookshelf – Unlocks at Lv 5, gives +5% global XP bonus.
  5. Cozy Fireplace – Expensive but worth it for crop growth speed.
